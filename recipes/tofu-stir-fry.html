Spicy Tofu Stir-Fry

A quick and flavorful stir-fry with crispy tofu and fresh vegetables, perfect for a weeknight meal. This dish is great for a healthy and satisfying dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup stir-fry sauce
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, toss the tofu cubes with soy sauce and cornstarch until coated.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides. Remove from the pan.
  3. Add the bell pepper and broccoli to the same pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for one minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the stir-fry sauce and red pepper flakes. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Return the tofu to the pan and toss to coat with the sauce. Cook for 1-2 minutes until heated through.
  7. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
