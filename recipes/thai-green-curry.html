Authentic Thai Green Curry

A fragrant and spicy curry with a blend of coconut milk, green chilies, lemongrass, and fresh vegetables. This dish is vibrant, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 lb chicken or firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce or soy sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or wok over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Pour in about a quarter of the coconut milk and stir to combine with the paste. Cook until the mixture thickens slightly and the oil separates.
  3. Add the remaining coconut milk and the vegetable broth. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add the chicken or tofu, bell pepper, and green beans. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the protein is cooked through.
  5. Stir in the fish sauce and brown sugar. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  6. Add the fresh basil leaves just before serving. Serve hot with steamed jasmine rice.
