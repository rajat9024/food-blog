Chocolate Lava Cake

Rich, decadent chocolate cakes with a molten center that oozes out upon the first cut. This is a simple dessert that looks impressive and tastes amazing.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz unsalted butter
  • 4 oz bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour four small ramekins.
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate together in a heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water. Stir until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and salt until thick and pale.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture. Sift the flour over the batter and fold until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter among the prepared ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  7. Carefully invert each cake onto a plate and serve immediately.
