Classic Margherita Pizza

Homemade pizza with fresh basil, mozzarella, and a tangy tomato sauce. Simple yet incredibly delicious, this classic Italian recipe is perfect for a fun night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually 500°F or 260°C).
  2. On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices and basil leaves on top. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
